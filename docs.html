What is SKINDEX?

SKINDEX is a token backed by a vault of CSGO skins. It operates as a decentralized, ETF-style asset on Ethereum, providing trustless and liquid exposure to in-game items.

Tokenomics

  • Mint with ETH
  • Burn to redeem ETH
  • Maximum Fees: 1% developer, 5% mint, 5% burn (adaptive, further details on the roadmap)
  • Burn fees are high at first to encourage long term holding

How Minting Works

Users can send ETH to the contract. The contract mints SKINDEX based on NAV minus mint fee and developer fee.

How Burning Works

Users can burn SKINDEX to redeem their share of the Vault value, calculated from NAV and minus burn fee. Redemption pulls from the ETH reserve pool.

Price Stabilization (Coming Soon)

When SKINDEX trades below NAV, the protocol uses BTC to buy back and reduce supply. When above, it sells into the market to capture value and grow the skin vault.

Oracle & Transparency

All skin deposits are tracked on-chain via bot-oracle reports. BTC and ETH prices come from Chainlink or similar. Vault values and composition are always public and queryable.

Security

  • Open-source contracts
  • Audits planned before mainnet
  • Bot-controlled skin custody with smart contract access only
