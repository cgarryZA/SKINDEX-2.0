Governance Overview

Governance will allow SKINDEX holders to shape the future of the protocol. Token holders will propose and vote on changes such as fee adjustments, vault strategy, or DAO treasury usage.

DAO Proposal System

  • Anyone with >1% of supply can create proposals
  • Each SKINDEX token = 1 vote
  • Minimum quorum: 10%
  • Proposal types: fee updates, bot access control, expansion votes

Example Governance Actions

Votes may be held for:

  • Changing mint or burn fee %
  • Adding support for Rust or Dota skins
  • Updating oracle price feed strategies

How to Participate

  1. Hold SKINDEX in a connected wallet
  2. Visit governance portal (launching soon)
  3. Vote on active proposals

Governance launch is planned for Phase 2 after mainnet stabilization.
